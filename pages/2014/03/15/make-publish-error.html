make pulish 时遇见的错误

    (pyenv2.7)[vic@fedora-vic vi-pelican]$ make publish
    pelican /home/vic/develop/vc/git/vi-pelican/content -o /home/vic/develop/vc/git/vi-pelican/output -s /home/vic/develop/vc/git/vi-pelican/publishconf.py
    ERROR: Skipping ./first_blog.md: could not find information about 'title'
    Done: Processed 0 articles and 0 pages in 0.99 seconds.

原因：.md 文档格式错误。

-需要定义 Title、date、Category、Tags、Slug、Author、Summary
-参考本文档的定义格式

参考官方文档
